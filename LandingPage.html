VolunteerConnect

VolunteerConnect is the bridge to recruit skilled volunteers for your nonprofit and voluntary tasks. We pair you up with individuals who are dedicated to and passionate about your cause and who can offer assistance as needed. And because volunteers are often donors as well, we make it easy for them to contribute their time and money.
